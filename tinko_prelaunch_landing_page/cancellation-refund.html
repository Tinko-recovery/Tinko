1. Pay-As-You-Go Model

Tinko operates on a strictly performance-based "Pay-As-You-Go" model. This means:

  • There are no upfront setup fees.
  • There are no monthly or annual subscription charges.
  • We only charge a small commission fee when we successfully recover a failed payment for you.

2. Cancellation Policy

Since there are no recurring subscriptions, you are not bound by any long-term contracts.

  • You may stop using Tinko at any time by simply disabling the service in your dashboard.
  • There are no cancellation fees or penalties for discontinuing the service.
  • Upon disabling the service, no further recovery attempts will be made, and no further fees will be incurred.

3. Refund Policy

Because our fees are only charged after a successful service delivery (i.e., recovering a payment), traditional refunds for "subscription time" do not apply.

Commission Fees

Commission fees charged on successful recoveries are generally non-refundable, as the service (the recovery of funds) has been successfully completed.

Exceptions & Billing Errors

We value fairness. Refunds on commission fees may be considered in the following cases:

  • Billing Errors: If you believe you were charged a commission in error (e.g., for a transaction that was not actually recovered), please contact us immediately for a full refund of the fee.
  • Duplicate Charges: Any duplicate charges will be refunded instantly upon verification.
