CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

Last Updated: 29 Nov 2025

1. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription to Tinko at any time. Cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

  • To cancel, please log in to your dashboard and navigate to Settings > Billing.
  • Alternatively, you can email us at support@tinko.in.
  • Once cancelled, you will not be charged for the next billing cycle.

2. Refund Policy

We strive to provide the best service possible. However, if you are not satisfied, our refund policy is as follows:

Monthly Subscriptions

We do not offer refunds for partial months of service. If you cancel mid-month, your access will continue until the end of the billing period.

Annual Subscriptions

If you cancel an annual subscription within the first 30 days, you are eligible for a full refund. After 30 days, refunds are pro-rated based on the remaining full months.

Exceptions

Refunds may be granted in exceptional circumstances, such as:

  • Service downtime exceeding our SLA.
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges.

3. Processing of Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original payment method.
